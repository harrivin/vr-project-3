Description and Motivation

The project presented is a Virtual Reality visualization of the dataset on the GDP per capita income(all races) in the United States from the year 1967-2014. The project begins with a scene that contains a house and a garage, The objects in the house and garage are set to active/inactive with the change in slider, the slider is used to denote a particular year and the text shows the GDP per capita of that particular year.

The motivation behind the project is to visualize a dataset and predict how the standard of living(material goods) of an average person living the United States changes from year to year based on the GDP per capita income.

The given experience uses first person perspective, head tracking and stereoscopic rendering to put the user in the Visualization scene. The user can move around the scene using a X-box Joystick(left stick) and can change the value of the slider(years) using "B" and "X" buttons.

Dataset Used

The Dataset used is from the Current Population Survey and contains the Per Capita Income of All Races from 1967 - 2014 Link :- http://www.census.gov/hhes/www/income/data/historical/people/2014/p01AR.xls

Intro

The Participants where told what GDP is and how the GDP per capita income is calculated

Gross domestic product (GDP) is the monetary value of all the finished goods and services produced within a country's(US) borders in a specific time period(a year in this case)

GDP per capita income = GDP/ total population of that specific region
